Privacy Policy — Air Strike Arcade

Effective Date: February 14, 2026 | Last Updated: February 14, 2026

1. Corporate Declaration and Scope

This Privacy Policy governs the data practices of AFK GAMES STUDIO (“the Studio,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) regarding the mobile game Air Strike Arcade (the “Service”). The Studio operates under a strict Data Minimization Protocol. We do not require account registration, and we do not directly collect or store Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as legal names, physical addresses, or contact credentials.

2. Privacy by Design: Local Data & ZERO Protocol AI

The Service utilizes local device storage (SharedPreferences) to save game progress and preferences. This data remains strictly on your device and is not transmitted to our servers:

  • Game Statistics: Encrypted local records of wins, losses, and combat history.
  • ZERO Protocol AI Data: Tactical combat patterns learned by the AI opponent are stored and processed locally.
  • User Preferences: Local settings for haptics, music, and sound effects.

Wipe Memory: Users may exercise their right to data erasure at any time via the "Wipe Memory" option in the main menu settings, which permanently purges all local records.

3. Automated Third-Party Processing

To provide a functional and monetized experience, the Service integrates Enterprise SDKs provided by Google:

  • Google Play Services: Facilitates core application functionality.
  • Google AdMob: May collect Advertising IDs (AAID), IP addresses (for approximate location), and ad interaction data to serve interest-based advertisements.

4. Global Regulatory Compliance (GDPR & CCPA)

We facilitate your statutory privacy rights:

  • Opt-Out: Users may reset or delete their Advertising ID via Settings > Google > Ads.
  • Data Retention: Local data is retained until manual erasure or app uninstallation. Google-collected advertising data is retained per Google's statutory policies (typically 14-26 months).
  • Children’s Privacy: This Service is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (COPPA) or 16 (GDPR).

5. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Florida, USA. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in Orange County, Florida.
