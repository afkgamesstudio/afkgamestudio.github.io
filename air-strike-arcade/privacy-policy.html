1. SCOPE; ENTITY; ROLES
This Privacy Policy describes how AFK GAMES STUDIO (“Studio,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) processes information in connection with the mobile application Air Strike Arcade (the “App”).
The App is designed as an offline-first experience. We do not require account registration, and we do not directly request or collect information such as legal names, physical addresses, or account credentials.
However, the App uses third-party SDKs (including Google AdMob and Google Play Services) that may process identifiers and technical information (which may be considered “personal data” or “personal information” under certain laws) as described below.

2. INFORMATION PROCESSED ON YOUR DEVICE (OFFLINE-FIRST)

2.1 Local Game Data (ZERO Data). The App stores certain information locally on your device to provide core functionality for single-player gameplay, including CPU/AI difficulty and behavior data (the “ZERO” data) processed on-device.

2.2 User Controls (Local Deletion). Because the App is offline-first, App data described in Section 2.1 is stored locally on your device. You can delete local App data by:
Using the App’s in-game deletion control (e.g., a “Wipe Memory” / “Reset” button available in the main menu); and/or
Clearing the App’s storage/data via your device settings (typically: Settings → Apps → Air Strike Arcade → Storage → Clear storage/Clear data); and/or
Uninstalling the App, subject to your device and operating system backup settings.
Note: Clearing cache may remove temporary files but may not remove all locally stored App data, depending on OS behavior.

3. INFORMATION PROCESSED BY THIRD PARTIES (AUTOMATED COLLECTION)
The App integrates third-party SDKs for advertising and platform functionality. These third parties may automatically collect or receive certain information, which may include:
Advertising/Device Identifiers: Google Advertising ID (or equivalent identifiers), device identifiers, and app instance identifiers.
Network/Location Signals: IP address and approximate location inferred from IP.
Device/Technical Data: device model, hardware/OS version, language, time zone, App version, diagnostics, and performance data.
Advertising Data: ad impressions, clicks, ad interaction events, frequency capping signals, and anti-fraud signals.
We do not operate our own analytics or advertising servers for these functions; instead, third parties process this information under their own terms and policies and, in some cases, as independent controllers.

4. HOW INFORMATION IS USED
We use (or enable third parties to use) information to:
Provide and maintain App functionality (e.g., local gameplay operation and local data deletion controls).
Display, measure, and optimize ads; prevent fraud and abuse; enforce policy compliance.
Maintain security and stability (e.g., troubleshooting, crash/performance diagnostics).
Comply with legal obligations.
